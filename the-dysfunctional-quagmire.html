The dysfunctional quagmire of the status quo

Vicki Langford February 2, 2024

To leave the comfort of the old sh*t requires some gumption and the willingness to let go of old opinions.

Which kinda sums up my day. Which wraps up another layer of the life I'm moving on from.

These are not easy things.
They are uncomfortable and these actions tend to upset the dysfunctional quagmire of status quo

-huh- 
-what-
I was just rambling writing, and damn.
"The dysfunctional quagmire of the status quo"...

Fkn love that line.
