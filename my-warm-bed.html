My Warm Bed

Vicki Langford January 15, 2024

I've made it to my warm bed.

Curating ones bed.

The bed itself. soft, firm or somewhere in-between

The sheets. sheen, softness, texture

The covers. heaviness, thickness, layers

The pillows. firmness, flexibility, quantity

Curled up or sprawled across

It's a very personal space

Our comfortable space.
Comfy cozy
Warm snuggly
Sleepy drifting

Safe
