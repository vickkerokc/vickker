Too much talking

Vicki Langford September 19, 2022

We made small talk
We shared stories
We talked opinions
We shared philosophy

We laughed and joked
We teased delightfully

I had fun but
The only thing wrong with last night
Was too much talking

Talking is overrated
Talking is a waste of breath
Lets save our breath for a kiss

Let's put our mouths together
Breathe in and out

We conversated
Discussing decisions like
Should we go to the club tonight
Or just stay home

We talked to friends
We talked to strangers

I had fun but
The only thing wrong with last night
Was too much talking

Talking is overrated
Talking is a waste of breath
Lets save our breath for a kiss

Let's put our mouths together
Breathe in and out

We discussed work
We worried about money
While we dreamed of vacations
Lifestyles of the rich and famous

We discussed the future
We analysed the past

You know what was wrong with last night
Too much talking.

Talking is overrated
Talking is a waste of breath
Lets save our breath for a kiss

The night dragged on and on
Too much talking

I'm in my head too much
There's a lot of noise in there

Just hold me now
Kissing is better than words.
