The Human Harmony

Vicki Langford January 28, 2024

I'm raw material
Ready to shape shift
Aware of the layers
Taking on shapes
To navigate experience
To experience life

There's a pattern
A resonance to our life
To the human condition
We are played
Or we become a player

Awareness of the patterns
Allows us to navigate the layers
The more aware
The more the symphony plays
The beautiful symphony of life
The chords their harmony
Their dissonance

The emotional chords of love and loss
Seeking and wanting connection
Slightly out of tune

Ah, but life is a self correcting chorus
And we all have our beautiful part to play
