May 9 2019

This concept was born.  
Healing the trauma and dogma around physical touch

Touching is taboo.  We fear our sensual selves.  We recoil from touch.  We have beliefs that don't serve us. We need touch.  We need physical release. Theres no right or wrong to how often. Non-consensual is not ok.  Manipulation of someone vulnerable is not ok. Girls and women need to be taught that we don't have to do what we are told all the time. Girls and women // boys too / need tools to prevent sexual abuse. 

Vicki Langford July 15, 2025
