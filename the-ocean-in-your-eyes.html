The Ocean in Your Eyes

Vicki Langford August 18, 2024

I’m swimming in the ocean in your eyes
It is vast and wide
Forever it holds me
I float in that ocean

The waves carry me
Gentle breezes
Swaying to and fro

Even when seas get stormy the sun shines brightly again
And I’m still there floating in the ocean of your eyes

It is a very comfortable place
Familiar and safe
I’ve been here before caught in the ocean in your eyes

Your ocean is expansive
I’m a mere drop of water
Part of something bigger than myself

The ocean in your eyes is no ones to contain, to control
I’m in awe and wonder

I see myself in the ocean in your eyes
I wail up in tears
My eyes weep a drop and we swirl together
Not knowing where one begins and the other ends

Blissfully in wonderment, I witness the beauty and grace
The power and force of the ocean in your eyes
Mesmerized

I shake and tremble with ever-flowing love
The ocean in your eyes belongs to no one

Not everyone can see it
Nor comprehend the grandeur
Yet you offer it freely

The ocean in your eyes knows no lack, no scarcity, no limits

Boundless, formless, space, energy, infinity
The ocean in your eyes reminds me we are not alone
We are part of so much more

I see also pain in the ocean in your eyes
This conduit that transmits so easily the frequencies of everyone, everything
The ocean in your eyes is a beautiful reflection back to those who are ready to see.
I see myself in the ocean in your eyes in a way I’ve never seen before.

I choose to see, to look, to feel and do my own work to heal

You are safe behind the reflection of the ocean in your eyes
You see people looking there, you see what they see, safely behind the reflection

No one can own the ocean in your eyes
They may try to siphon some for themselves or take a bucket full out

The ocean in your eyes is abundance, your connection to source
Ever present, it washes over you, it grounds you when you let it

Thank you for showing me the ocean in your eyes
For letting me swim and swirl
Feel my respect and awe, my deep admiration

For the ocean in your eyes does not belong to me, to anyone

The ocean in your eyes is freedom
Let the ocean breeze guide your waves
Your connection to source, your compass
