The Vickker

Vickker isn't a nickname. Only two people have ever called me that. And I realize now they saw in me the spiritual seeker that I am. Not a vicar, but a Vickker.

While a lot of these writings might seem heavy or deep, that is only one part of it. Humor and play are vitally important.  Emotions are waves of energy and they come and go like the tides. (Perhaps they too are impacted by moon :)

Today, Vickker, is like a persona for me. Maybe it might become one for you. Or if you've stumbled upon this site, I hope you find a morsel of what you seek. 

We each are our own spiritual seeker and healer. For me writing has been a tool to help me process my feelings and ultimately heal. 

Art and Heart

  1. Creators. I want to create awareness for artist I love. For the unseen, unheard.
  2. Create. I too am a creator and I want to share those creations.
  3. Collaborate. I want to work together with other creators.
  4. Rhythm. There's a harmony to life, a rhythm. If you don't like your life's symphony, its possible to create some new harmonies.
  5. Explore. Being ever curious, curious enough to actually listen.
