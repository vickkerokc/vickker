Art and Heart

  1. Creators. I want to create awareness for artist I love. For the unseen, unheard.
  2. Create. I too am a creator and I want to share those creations.
  3. Collaborate. I want to work together with other creators.
  4. Rhythm. There's a harmony to life, a rhythm. If you don't like your life's symphony, its possible to create some new harmonies.
  5. Explore. Being ever curious, curious enough to actually listen.
