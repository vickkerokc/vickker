Whirlwind

Vicki Langford June 2, 2024

I'm in a whirlwind
Tossing and turning
Flying high
Breaking barriers
Freeing my mind

Taking flight
Anything is possible
So many layers
Bottom up
Top down

My eyes are open
Seeing it all
Flying by
Grooving
Leaving the past

Reshaping
Rebuilding
Regenerating

I'm in a whirlwind
Moving fast
Taking it slow
Vibrating higher
Than I ever know

Life is meant to live
Living is being alive

Leave the old beliefs
Let the whirlwind
Carry you away
