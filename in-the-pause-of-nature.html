In the pause of nature

Vicki Langford July 7, 2023

Sitting in the window seat
the window open
listening to the wind
feeling the rain
watching for lightening flashes
hearing the thunder rumbles

the expansion of what we thought
about what we are
literally our energy field extends limitless

across the earth and beyond

infinity let me on
