Force Of Life

Vicki Langford June 22, 2023

Sometimes I'm a hurricane
Sometimes I'm a sunny day

I'm a force of nature
And can't be contained
Controlled

My energy courses through all life
We are one energy
Force of Life

Let the wind blow
And the thunder roar
Let it all wash over and through

Respect me
Be in awe of me

Sometimes I'm the rainbow
After the storm
Sometimes I'm the summer breeze

Sometimes my lightning crashes and
Causes damage

All my forms are necessary
They all serve a purpose

I'm all powerful and control nothing
I'm awe inspiring and seek no validation
